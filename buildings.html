Arete Smart Buildings

Arete transforms building operations by making each building's Arete the single trusted authority for its data, enabling consistent, secure management for all assets and applications.

Digital Confidence for Buildings

Imagine a commercial building where every system—lighting, HVAC, security, and facility apps—operates smoothly through a single source of truth.

That's the Arete advantage: shifting from siloed devices and scattered data to genuine operational consistency built for business reliability.

The Building's Digital Heart - Arete serving as a trusted orchestrator

The Building's Digital Heart

At the center of building's information management is Arete, serving as a trusted orchestrator for all connected systems. Facility apps, monitoring tools, utility meters, and security feeds connect either on-premises using IBB hosts or through secure cloud environments.

Arete's consistent behavior—secure, interoperable, resilient, and plug-n-play—lets business professionals manage operations with confidence, trusting every interaction to follow modern security and open standards.

If All Buildings Used Arete...

Once a building adopts Arete, the playbook for operational success becomes scalable. Now, imagine every building in your company's portfolio equipped with its own Arete.

Each Arete reliably manages its building's internal assets and coordinates with trusted external service providers, government-provided services, and utility grids, while also managing your fleet's portfolio, all using standards-based interactions securely.

Arete Across the Fleet

When Arete powers each property in a real estate portfolio, asset management transitions from isolated points to an integrated, context-aware system-of-systems.

Arete Across the Fleet - Integrated portfolio management

Arete units in different buildings can collaborate, share data where permitted, and connect to higher-level orchestrators, such as a city-wide smart city orchestrator, which itself is an Arete for the city, coordinating infrastructure, buildings, and transportation across the smart city.

This creates seamless pathways for government, service organizations, and business operators to dynamically optimize their interactions.

Flexibility for the Future

Arete isn't just a tool for today; its open-source, modular architecture means each building or portfolio can adapt quickly to new service providers, evolving regulations, or emerging technology trends. It's a living platform, where orchestration for provider and consumer roles shapes the future of operations, security, and business growth.
