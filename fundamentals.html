Understanding Core Arete Concepts

Arete organizes networks by focusing on nodes, context, and capabilities, providing a structured foundation for systems to work together securely and efficiently. Instead of building connections based on technical addresses, Arete emphasizes business roles and real-world relationships. Its architecture helps organizations define and manage how technology assets interact, keeping things organized, traceable, and easy to adapt as needs change.

This page aims to clarify the terminology and concepts that are central to Arete, presenting the basics in a way that helps any business leader or technical stakeholder understand how the system works.
