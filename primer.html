Project Arete

Mission

Empowering communities through democratized access to autonomous transportation.

Vision

A world where autonomous vehicles serve humanity's collective needs rather than corporate interests alone.

What We're Building

Open Source Autonomous Platform

  • Community-owned fleet management system
  • Transparent AI decision-making frameworks
  • Decentralized coordination protocols
  • Privacy-first rider experience

Why It Matters

Current autonomous vehicle development concentrates power and profit in the hands of a few corporations. Project Arete offers an alternative: technology that belongs to everyone, governed by community consensus, and designed to serve public good.
